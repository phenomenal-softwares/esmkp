MESSAGE FROM THE CHOIR COORDINATOR

ABOUT THE UNIT

"SUNRISE VOICES" is the name of the chapter choir unit, in collaboration with the Technical unit. We are responsible for the general church music ministration, instrumentals, and the sound output

Choir rehearsals are generally scheduled for Saturdays 4pm - 6pm. However, there may be certain variations in which the rehearsal might be fixed at any other day/time or suspended generally; factors responsible are Holidays, Exam periods, Special programmes, Choir outings, etc, as directed by the Choir Coordinator

  • SISTERS
    Fixing/painting of nails and lips, use of bangles, wearing of trousers and miniskirts are all absolutely prohibited
  • BROTHERS
    Use of crazy jeans, sagging of trousers, dyed hair, dreads, are all absolutely prohibited
  • Shoes or sandals are required in any church programme. Use of slippers are not allowed
  • You are expected to be humble, cheerful, friendly, and submissive to your leader

  • Absenteeism and lateness without prior notice is a serious offence. You are obligated to inform your leader if you will be absent in any programme
  • Noise and disturbance in any programme is punishable
  • Arrogance, insults or disrespect towards the church executives, members and other units is a very severe offence
  • Use of mobile phones or ringing of phones during any programme is punishable
